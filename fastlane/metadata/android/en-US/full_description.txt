<p><i>FocusLauncher</i> is a focus-first launcher that replaces your device's home screen and helps you use your phone more deliberately. It is a fork of <i>Kvaesitso</i>, reshaped around calmer surfaces, intentional app launches, and less impulsive screen time.</p>
<p>Instead of a busy, distraction-heavy home screen, FocusLauncher keeps things apps-first: a fast, on-device search centered on the apps you actually use, with no global web search or feeds pulling your attention away.</p>
<p>To support focus, you can:</p>
<ul>
<li>Separate essential apps from distracting ones with simple app classification.</li>
<li>Add explainable launch friction in front of distracting apps, so opening them is a choice rather than a reflex.</li>
<li>Run focus sessions with temporary unlocks and daily limits to keep distracting apps in check.</li>
<li>Review your focus history and weekly focus insights to see how your habits change over time.</li>
<li>Stay grounded with Time Awareness, which surfaces periodic reminders of how long you have been on your device.</li>
<li>Jot things down instantly with Quick Capture notes, kept locally and shared only when you choose.</li>
</ul>
<p>FocusLauncher is local-first and privacy-respecting: your app list, usage data, focus state, and notes stay on your device. There are no accounts and no analytics.</p>
